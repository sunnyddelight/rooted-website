Our Story

Why
Rooted?

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, everything begins at the root — gēn (根). When we nourish what's foundational — our energy, our digestion, our body — harmony follows naturally. That's hé (和).

For some of us, these ideas are woven into childhood memories: a pot of herbal soup on the stove, a grandmother's quiet insistence that food is medicine. For others, it's a new discovery — an ancient wisdom that makes us slow down and reconnect with our bodies in a world that moves too fast.

Rooted is for both. We don't ask you to believe anything. We simply offer what generations before us already knew: that the best health is nourished slowly, from the root up.

Every product is crafted from traditional formulations passed down through generations — adapted for modern life. Real ingredients, real tradition, real care.

About This Blend

If you've been feeling bloated, heavy, or more tired than usual — if your digestion has slowed down or your body just feels a little off — you're not alone. In Traditional Chinese Medicine, these are recognized as signs of dampness accumulating in the body, and they're more common than you might think.

Our Dampness Adieu blend is thoughtfully crafted according to TCM principles, where each ingredient is traditionally valued for its role in restoring balance. Astragalus root and Chinese yam are traditionally used to support the spleen — considered in TCM to be the organ most responsible for healthy fluid metabolism. Roasted rice gently warms the digestive system, while gorgon fruit and phaseolous bean have long been used in Chinese herbalism to promote healthy fluid balance. Jujube dates and dried longan are prized for replenishing qi and blood, and goji berries are traditionally believed to nourish the liver and kidneys.

Together, these ingredients reflect centuries of herbal wisdom — working in harmony to support your body's natural balance. Pair it with warm, nourishing foods and a walk in the sun to bid that dampness adieu.

About This Blend

Our ancestors understood something timeless: that true beauty is nourished from within. In Traditional Chinese Medicine, radiant skin and a healthy glow are believed to come from nourishing yin, enriching the blood, and keeping qi flowing freely — and that's the philosophy behind Beauty Brew.

At the heart of this blend is snow fungus, traditionally treasured as nature's collagen — believed to deeply hydrate and support skin elasticity from the inside out. Goji berries are traditionally used to nourish the liver and brighten the complexion, while jujube dates are valued in TCM for tonifying qi and blood to promote a healthy, natural glow. Lotus seeds are believed to calm the spirit and strengthen the spleen, helping your body absorb and distribute nourishment. A touch of rock sugar gently moistens and balances the blend's warmth, tying everything together into a subtly sweet, restorative harmony.

Enjoy it as an afternoon pick-me-up or a comforting nighttime treat. This isn't skincare in a bottle — it's nourishment in a bowl.

About This Blend

Start your morning grounded. In Traditional Chinese Medicine, a strong morning meal is believed to warm the spleen and stomach, nourish your essence, and set a steady foundation of energy for the day — and that's the philosophy behind every packet of Morning Mantra.

Whole grain oatmeal forms the base, offering sustained fiber and gentle warmth to support comfortable digestion. Black sesame powder is a prized ingredient in TCM, traditionally valued for nourishing the kidneys and liver and replenishing jing — your body's deepest reserves of vitality. Goji berries round out the blend, traditionally used to tonify the liver and kidneys while adding a touch of natural sweetness.

Together, these three ingredients work in balance: oatmeal for grounding, black sesame for deep nourishment, and goji berries for gentle vitality. No crash, no bloat — just a warm, rooted start to whatever comes next.
